Leadership

Feminism is the belief that there should be equality between genders in all aspects of society. When an unjust power dynamic does exist, the return to justice is three-fold:

  1. Redesign cultural norms and expectations of gender to be fair.
  2. Reallocate representation within the system to be balanced.
  3. Redistribute unearned privilege within the system to be just.

—

Technology isn't gender neutral. The intent we imbue upon it, the effects our interpretations of it have on society, not to mention the relational environments in which we develop it all factor into the equality between genders. As software engineers and developers we need to navigate and negotiate our technological uses and productions and be socially aware of how they can be made to undermine the rights that uphold gender equality.
